About FLL Submerged

FLL Submerged is a global initiative that aims to raise awareness about the threats facing marine life and the oceans. It encourages innovation and creativity through its challenges to inspire the next generation of environmental leaders. By using robotics and engineering, FLL teams engage with ocean-related problems, seeking solutions to protect marine ecosystems and ensure sustainable practices.

The FLL Submerged program also focuses on educating the public about the importance of preserving ocean biodiversity and finding ways to reduce pollution, tackle climate change, and promote sustainable fishing practices.

Challenges Faced by Ocean Animals

  • Pollution

    Plastic waste and toxins endanger marine ecosystems.

  • Climate Change

    Rising temperatures and acidification threaten biodiversity.

  • Overfishing

    Destructive fishing practices deplete marine populations.

  • Habitat Destruction

    Human activities degrade critical marine environments.
